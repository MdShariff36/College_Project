Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a car?

Go to the Cars page, select your vehicle, and follow the booking form. You can also use Quick booking on the home page.

What documents do I need?

Valid driver's license and government-issued ID. For long-term rentals we may require additional proof of address.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes — cancellations and modifications depend on the selected plan. Contact support for changes.
